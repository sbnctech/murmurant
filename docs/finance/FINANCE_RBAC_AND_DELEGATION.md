# Finance RBAC & Delegation Model

## Principles
- Least privilege
- Explicit escalation
- No implicit authority

## Role Capabilities
### Finance Manager
- Validate
- Reject
- Request clarification

### VP of Finance
- Approve within budget and threshold
- Cannot override policy

### President
- Full approval authority
- Cannot bypass audit

### Board
- Required for defined categories

## Delegation Rules
- No self-approval
- No cross-domain delegation
- Time-bounded grants
