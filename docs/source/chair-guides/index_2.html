Key Findings Summary

Important Context: All the "interesting example" sites analyzed are native Wild Apricot sites, not WordPress integrations. This is actually ideal for SBNC since it shows what's achievable within the platform you're already using.

Native Wild Apricot sites can be highly effective without custom design investment. Success comes from:

  • Clear communication of purpose and benefits
  • Easy navigation to key information
  • Multiple engagement channels (newsletter, social media, events)
  • Fresh, updated content
  • Strong calls-to-action

WordPress + Wild Apricot integration adds significant complexity and cost without necessarily solving SBNC's identified UX challenges. Better to optimize within the native platform.

These analyses are based on:

  • Sites mentioned in Wild Apricot forums/blog or showcased by design firms
  • Sites that could be accessed and verified at time of analysis (November 2025)
  • Subjective assessment of "interesting features"

This is not a comprehensive scientific survey or expert-curated "best of" list. Rather, it's an illustrative sample to inform SBNC's design thinking process.
